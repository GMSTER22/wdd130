Overview

Purpose

The purpose is to display our expertise in pop up picnics. We plan on showcasing how we can put together a great picnics with 3 differents themes for our customers to enjoy, we are talking fancy picnics, luxury picnics. We want to attract people that are interested in spending some quality time with their partner without any effort, we want them to feel special in a great environment.

Audience

Our target audience are couples, from teenagers to old people who are looking for a unique experience. They are couples who are in love and trying to spend some quality time with their partner. It can be either the boyfriend/husband or girlfriend/wife who wants to suprise his/her other half with a romantic, luxury picnic.
